Incoterm Strategy: Why FOB?

Our choice of Incoterm is critical for balancing cost, control, and risk.

FOB (Free On Board) - Chosen

  • Maximum Control: We (the buyer) select the ocean carrier and control the main freight.
  • Cost Efficiency: Allows negotiation for competitive freight rates, avoiding inflated seller-side costs.
  • Insurance Flexibility: We choose our own marine insurance, ensuring better coverage at lower premiums.
  • Clear Risk Transfer: Risk transfers from seller to buyer once goods are loaded on the vessel.

CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight) - Alternative

  • Seller arranges and pays for freight and insurance to the destination port.
  • More convenient for the buyer, but offers less control and is often more expensive.
  • Risk of hidden costs in the seller's final price.

Port Selection: Antwerp vs. Rotterdam

A strategic decision to select the most specialized port for our high-value commodity.

Port of Antwerp (Chosen)

  • World's #1 Cocoa Port: Largest storage capacity for cocoa in the world.
  • Specialized Infrastructure: Climate-controlled warehouses dedicated to cocoa.
  • Quality Control Hub: On-site services for sampling, grading, and fumigation.
  • Superior Swiss Connectivity: Excellent, direct rail and barge links to Basel.

Port of Rotterdam

  • Europe's largest overall port by cargo volume.
  • Excellent general container handling facilities.
  • Good connectivity, but less specialized for cocoa.
  • Potentially longer transit to Switzerland.

The Verdict

Antwerp's specialization in cocoa provides unparalleled expertise, ensures quality preservation, and offers the most efficient onward logistics to Switzerland. This de-risks the supply chain and justifies its selection over Rotterdam, despite Rotterdam's larger overall size.

Source: Port of Antwerp-Bruges. (2024). Annual Report.

Risk Analysis & Mitigation Plan

Transportation Delays

Port congestion, customs holds, or bad weather.
[Impact: Medium, Likelihood: Medium]

Mitigation: Build in buffer stock (3-5 days).

Price Volatility

Commodity market price fluctuations.
[Impact: High, Likelihood: Medium]

Mitigation: Secure long-term supplier contracts.

Quality Degradation

Moisture or improper handling during transit.
[Impact: High, Likelihood: Low]

Mitigation: Use ventilated containers and cargo insurance.

Currency Fluctuation

Changes in the USD/CHF exchange rate.
[Impact: Medium, Likelihood: High]

Mitigation: Use forward currency contracts.
